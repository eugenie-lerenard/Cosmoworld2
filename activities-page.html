COSMOWORLD Music Activities Passes

Activities

A music festival isn’t just about the music, it’s also about having the greatest time in the world, encounter new people and dance till your legs can’t hold you anymore ! And, to make sure of it, we panned a lot of different activities that will take place during the festival.
Take a look !

DJ Booths

DJ’s from all accross the world and with different styles will
be there, in separate booths, making sure you are having the
time of your lives ! So, if you want to take a break from
massive crowds and enjoy yourself shut out from the outside
world, this is where you will be

Fireworks

If you decide to be here Friday night, you will have the
opportunity to asist to one of the most beautiful
fireworks you

A Holi Festival

Our version of the famous Indian Holy Festival will take place
during the course of Saturday ! Pink, blue, yellow, green
powders and a huge crowd of people ... It will be an explosion
of colors that will mark your spirit forever !

Relaxing Areas

Dancing, standing and walking all day can be exhausting we
know... But don’t worry we didn’t forget about you ! During
the entire festival, you will be able to find relaxing spots all
around site. Thus, you will be able to take a breath, recharge
and get ready for what happens next

Merch Stands

The Merch of your favorite artists, as well as exclusive pieces
from the festival, will be at your disposal at the
merchandising stands. They will be located at the front gate,
so you won’t forget to buy yours on your way out !

Ferris Wheel

A music festival won’t be a music festival without a big
ferries wheel, we know ... But, we’ve got you ! For this year
edition, we went over the limits and we made it happen. So,
make sure you take a ride before the end !
